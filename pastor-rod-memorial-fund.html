Pastor Rod Memorial Fund

Dear San Lo Family and Community,

We continue to be saddened by the passing of our dear brother and Pastor Rod on Good Friday. We will miss his warm greetings, his biblical and transparent teaching, and his firm leadership and guidance.

Pastor Rod was a fighter in all aspects of life and ministry especially as he battled cancer the past year and half. He has left us with an everlasting impression and testimony of God’s power as he overcame several close calls during his fight. Rod is in his heavenly home with His Father. What acomfort that he is experiencing this verse…which has carried us…as our theme.

2 Corinthians 4:17

“For this light momentary affliction is preparing for us an eternal weight of glory beyond all comparison.”

Koko and Annie Irene want to extend their deep gratitude for your continued love and support. They are filled with joy to know that Rod is home with the Lord. They also want you to know that they are extremely grateful for all your generosity especially all the cards, emails, and flowers. At this time, they request that you refrain from sending any more flowers as their home is full with your love.

If you would like to support them and continue to honor Pastor Rod, the family and church has set up a memorial fund in Pastor Rod’s honor. This memorial fund has been set up to support two important causes that were close to Rod’s heart:

Crisis Fund - Even in his final weeks, Rod wanted to support people going through the COVID-19 crisis especially those who were in financial need. Under Pastor Rod’s leadership, he used this crisis fund to care for others in a financial crisis.

GROW SANLO Campaign - a hallmark of Pastor Rod’s ministry was the creation of the GROW SANLO campaign to raise funds for a new church facility. Under Pastor Rod’s leadership, he grew the church to place beyond its capacity, and he had the vision to expand the church and the gospel beyond its current facility.

If you would like to extend your support, you may do so here at www.slzjcc.org and click the giving button in the upper right corner. This will send you to our secure giving page, and then choose the Fund - Memorial Fund - In Honor of Pastor Rod.

This memorial fund will help carry on Pastor Rod’s legacy to make an eternal impact on people for generations to come. The family and San Lo family appreciates your generosity and love.

In His Honor,
Pastor Eric (on behalf of Koko and Annie Irene)
