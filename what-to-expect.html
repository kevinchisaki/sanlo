What To Expect?

San Lorenzo Japanese Christian Church gathers for worship every Sunday. We provide four different services. Here’s what you’ll find at San Lo:

A MULTI-GENERATIONAL CHURCH

You’ll see people of all ages, and we come together as one family as believers in Jesus Christ.

FRIENDLY PEOPLE

We’d love to meet you, so feel free to introduce yourself. Please let us know what we can do to care for you while you’re here.

A RELAXED ATMOSPHERE

You’ll notice some folks who like to dress up for church and others who don’t. Feel free to wear what’s comfortable for you, and you’ll fit right in.

JOYFUL WORSHIP

Our Sundays include contemporary music, sharing, prayer, and relevant biblical preaching from the Bible.

First time to San Lo?

If you are new to San Lo and you plan to visit on a Sunday, then here are some practical things to know before you come:

SERVICES

We have seperate Sunday morning Worship Services which are conducted in both English and Japanese.

PARKING

Our parking lot is assigned for visitors, seniors, and families with young children. If you can’t find a parking spot, then there is parking available across the street in the Food Maxx parking lot.

CHILDCARE

Parents can attend the services while their children attend Nursery or Children’s Church.

THE LAST SUNDAYS

Every last Sunday of the month, we have a combined service between our 1st and 2nd service at 10:45am. We also provide a fellowship hour of snacks after service. It is a great time to unite our church in fellowship and get to know each other.
