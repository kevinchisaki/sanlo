Missions

At SLZJCC, we desire to be a church spreading the gospel to all nations and people groups. In doing so, we support various missionaries and organization serving in various places across the globe. See our missions page and prayer guide for more information.

View our monthly prayer guide for our missionaries.

International Student Fellowship (ISF)

At SLZJCC, we desire to reach our world and community, so several of our members reach out to international students on the nearby campus of Cal State East Bay through a ministry called International Student Fellowship. They provide different activities for international students like Friday English Fellowship, basketball ministry, weekend sightseeing, and much more.

Vacation Bible School (VBS)

Every July, we provide a week-long vacation bible school for children from pre-school to Jr. High to have fun and learn about Jesus.

Please contact Gloria Kama for more information.

3-on-3 Basketball Tournament

Every August, we have a 3-on-3 basketball tournament for all ages and every skill level at the Alameda Naval Air Base Gym. We have eight different divisions for every person.

See our Youth Ministry page for more information.

Harvest Festival

Every October, we provide an alternative to Halloween called Harvest Festival. It is a safe and fun environment for children of all ages to play games and to win prizes. We have a bible story time during every harvest festival as well.
